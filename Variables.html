Math 8 Week 3-5 Tutorial

Examples of Dependent and Independent Variables

1. Alvin is counting the amount of paper he needs to print his activity in EsP.

  • Dependent: The amount of paper needed.
  • Independent: The amount of pages in the activity document.

2. A pilot is calculating the amount of fuel for his plane adjusted by his estimated arrival to his destination as to reduce unnecessary weight.

  • Dependent: The amount of fuel.
  • Independent: The distance of his destination.

3. A 'plantita' waters her plants to grow them

  • Dependent: The height of the plants.
  • Independent: The amount of water used.
